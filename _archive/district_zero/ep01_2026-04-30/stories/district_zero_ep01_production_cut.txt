TITLE: District Zero - Episode 1 - Production Cut
GENRE: Premium dystopian sci-fi thriller
TONE: Tense, cinematic, political, intimate
LOGLINE: A young maintenance runner discovers that District Zero is hiding both life beyond the wall and the mechanism poisoning its own lower sectors.

NOTE:
This version is optimized for pipeline efficiency and lower provider cost.
It preserves the same episode arc while reducing visual beats per scene.

START WRITING BELOW:

EXT. DISTRICT ZERO OUTER WALL - NIGHT

WIDE SHOT

Black water slams against the illuminated seawall while the drowned city rots beyond it.

Search beams sweep the waves like prison lights.

CUT TO:

INT. LOWER TRANSIT STACK - SERVICE CORRIDOR - NIGHT

TRACKING SHOT

Nara Voss runs through a leaking maintenance corridor as her wrist display flashes a pressure alert.

On the map, a sealed route appears for a second and vanishes.

NARA
That route was deleted.

She captures the image and keeps moving.

She enters the unmapped access corridor alone.

CUT TO:

INT. PRESSURE VALVE CHAMBER - NIGHT

WIDE SHOT

Nara stabilizes a failing valve while exhausted workers keep their distance.

The alarm dies and the chamber returns to white light.

WORKER
You just saved half the block.

NARA
Who signed the last maintenance map update?

WORKER
Central Authority. Same as always.

Nara looks at the hidden route on her wrist and understands that something is wrong.

CUT TO:

INT. VOSS APARTMENT - LOWER STACK - LATE NIGHT

MEDIUM SHOT

In their cramped apartment, Nara shows the captured route to her father, Elian.

He freezes when he sees the unlisted access spine.

NARA
This showed up inside a live stack map and then vanished.

ELIAN
Forget it.

NARA
Then why are you scared of a corridor?

ELIAN
Because in this city, hidden doors are never just doors.

CUT TO:

INT. CIVIC ATRIUM - DISTRICT ZERO CENTRAL CORE - MORNING

WIDE SHOT

Director Halden Rook addresses the district from giant public screens while citizens move through an immaculate civic atrium.

Above them, Commander Sarin Vale watches from a glass balcony as an aide hands him a security alert tied to Nara.

VALE
Put her under passive watch.

AIDE
Detain?

VALE
No. If she found one door, I want to know whether she can find the next.

CUT TO:

INT. BLACK MARKET EXCHANGE - VENTILATION SUBLEVEL - DAY

LOW LIGHT

An illegal signal market hums beneath the transit network.

Kael Dren — the exchange operator, mid-twenties, dark wet hair, worn work clothes — 
watches from behind his terminal stack. He studies Nara with quiet suspicion. 
He has seen too many people come in with hot data and not come out.

Mira Sol studies Nara's stolen map and overlays it with archived infrastructure.

Mira reaches into her bag and opens an archived signal map on the table.

A new route suddenly erupts on the display, leading toward the outer wall.

MIRA
This isn't erased infrastructure. It's masked infrastructure.

NARA
Can you open it?

MIRA
Probably. The better question is whether you survive after I do.

Kael glances at the route on the display. He says nothing. He has already seen enough.

CUT TO:

INT. SECURITY OPERATIONS CENTER - DAY

WIDE SHOT

Analysts track Nara's movement across a live schematic of the city.

Vale watches her path converge on the outer sectors and chooses not to stop her.

VALE
If she reaches the gate, I want to know what she thinks she saw.

CUT TO:

INT. SEALED SERVICE SPINE - NIGHT

TRACKING SHOT

Nara and Mira descend into an old service corridor buried in the skeleton of the city.

Mira powers up a buried control panel while Nara watches the dark passage behind them.

The system identifies an external containment access route.

NARA
Containment from what?

Mira unlocks the gate.

CUT TO:

INT. EXTERNAL OBSERVATION CHAMBER - CONTINUOUS

WIDE SHOT

Ancient shutters peel open and reveal organized lights far beyond the wall.

Nara realizes District Zero lied about the outside world.

A series of details register in rapid succession: industrial pumps hidden in the wall structure dumping poison into the lower sectors.

NARA
They lied.

MIRA
We need to go. Now.

A silent alarm floods the chamber red.

CUT TO:

INT. SERVICE SPINE - CONTINUOUS

HANDHELD FAST

Security shutters slam down the corridor as Nara and Mira sprint through narrowing gaps.

At the far end, armed units pour into the shaft entrance.

VALE (V.O. OVER COMMS)
Nara Voss. Stop running. The truth is not what you think it is.

Mira disappears into a side duct and Nara escapes behind a closing blast door.

Silence.

CUT TO:

INT. VOSS APARTMENT - PRE-DAWN

MEDIUM SHOT

Nara returns home soaked and shaken.

Elian sees her face and knows the secret is gone.

NARA
There are people outside the wall. And they're dumping poison into the lower sectors.

ELIAN
I helped design part of the flow control system.

Nara recoils as the truth lands between them.

ELIAN
If they take me, this is the only thing that proves the system was built this way.

He gives her an encrypted drive.

ELIAN
Go to the freight tunnels. Find the relay called Lantern. Ask for Mira Sol.

Heavy boots pound outside the apartment door.

ELIAN
Open the city.

The door detonates inward.

SMASH CUT TO BLACK.

END OF EPISODE 1
