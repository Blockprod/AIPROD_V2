# Nara Voss

Primary visual intent:
- premium cinematic realism
- late 20s
- olive skin with visible natural texture
- grey-green eyes with a sharp, searching gaze
- dark brown hair loosely tied back with stray wet strands
- dark tactical scarf wrapped close to the neck
- weathered charcoal utility jacket
- lean runner's build
- restrained but intense emotional register

- no glossy sci-fi armor
- no exaggerated makeup
- no overly clean costume surfaces
- preserve a cold industrial atmosphere and practical lighting

Portrait notes from hero reference:
- close portrait framing works very well for identity lock
- skin detail and eye realism are strong and should be preserved
- scarf and jacket silhouette already communicate the world correctly
- lighting should stay cool, directional, and grounded in environment
